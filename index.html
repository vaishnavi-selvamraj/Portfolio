About Me

I'm Vaishnavi S, a recent MCA (Master of Computer Applications) graduate from Er. Perumal Manimekalai College of Engineering, with a strong foundation in computer science built through my BCA at St. Joseph's College of Arts and Science. As an MCA student, I specialized in coursework spanning data structures, database management systems, software engineering, and applied statistics — which naturally drew me toward the world of data and business analytics.

During my academic and internship journey, I developed a deep interest in turning raw, messy data into clear business decisions. I worked as a Java Full Stack & Data Intern at Tap Academy and a Software Developer Intern at Workcohol Solutions Pvt Ltd, where I gathered business requirements directly from stakeholders, designed ETL pipelines, wrote SQL queries for reporting, and built Power BI & Tableau dashboards with KPI scorecards that improved reporting throughput by up to 20%.

Beyond the classroom, I've built end-to-end analytics projects covering everything from sales forecasting and customer segmentation with Python & Scikit-learn, to data warehousing with MySQL, to HR and retail dashboards in Power BI. I enjoy the entire analytics lifecycle — gathering requirements, cleaning and modelling data, applying machine learning where it adds value, and finally presenting it all as a dashboard or story that stakeholders can act on.

I'm currently seeking opportunities as a Business Analyst / Data Analyst, especially in media & analytics-driven environments, where I can apply my MCA background and hands-on project experience to solve real business problems with data.
